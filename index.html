  • WELCOM TO EST BAKERY
    •
    • Established in 2004 at Ngo Tram street, Hoan Kiem district, Hanoi. Over 15 years of development, up to now, EST Bakery has 13 business establishments located on densely populated streets in Hanoi. EST Bakery products are made from imported ingredients from countries with traditional baking such as Newzeland, USA, France, Belgium. With delicious flavors typical of cream, butter, milk, cheese, almonds, chocolate... under the skillful hands of experienced bakers. The factory scale is more than 2000m2 with advanced and modern equipment according to ISO 2018 standards, the whole factory is covered with special EPOXY paint. EST Bakery always brings customers the best quality products, absolutely guaranteed on food safety and hygiene.
